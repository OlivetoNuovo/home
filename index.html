Welcome to OlivetoNuovo

OlivetoNuovo is a restoration project of an ancient olive grove destroyed by fire.

We are giving new life to a Sicilian Hill in Makari and a family's dream.

OlivetoNuovo Experience

OlivetoNuovo is a travel at the heart of Sicily to have a unique ecotourism experience in the village of Makari.

2020

A fire caused by arson go wild and destroy our olive grove. Makari olive grove was made by our grandfather in the '60 down by Zingaro Natural Reserve.

2025

Since 2021, we have been working with family, friends, and volunteers to recover the hill, create a special area for preservation, and cultivate olives agroecologically.
